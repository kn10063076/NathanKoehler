Welcome!

    This is the "All About Me" web page of the Nathan Koehler website. As you have likely guessed, my name is Nathan Koehler, and I am a Sophomore at The Colony High School. I don't have many extracurricular activities, but I am part of a program called "The Collegiate Academy." Another thing about me is that I am a former cheerleader; I was a cheerleader in 8th grade and Freshman years. After high school, this is what I would like to be.
