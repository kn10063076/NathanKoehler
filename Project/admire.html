Who I Admire

The person who I most admire is Bob Ross.


The reasons I admire Bob Ross so much are:
  • Bob was always kind, even to animals.
  • Bob was very calm towards everybody, and had a gentle demeanour.
  • Bob was a very talented artist, and I watch his show for guidance.
  • Not only was he kind to animals, he often nursed injured wild animals back to health.
Bob Ross was a very nice and calm man, and I respect him for that. He was like a rock at the bottom of a roaring river; he let what most people considered to be a big deal float past him. And for these reasons, I admire him.
